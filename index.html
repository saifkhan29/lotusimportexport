What We Export

Indian Spices

Indian spices are grown in different parts of the country, each with its unique soil and climate conditions that affect the flavor, aroma, and quality of the spice.

Pulses and Rice

LIndia produces a wide range of rice varieties, including Basmati and non-Basmati rice. Basmati rice is known for its unique aroma and flavor, and it is a high-value crop that is exported to many countries.

Grains Porducts

India is a major producer of different types of grains, owing to its diverse climate and fertile soil. The country produces a wide range of grains including rice, wheat, corn, millet, sorghum, barley, and pulses such as lentils, chickpeas, and kidney beans.

Fruits and Vegetables

We export a variety of fruits such as mangoes, bananas, grapes, pomegranates, and citrus fruits, as well as vegetables such as onions, potatoes, tomatoes, and okra.

Mechanical Seals

Mechanical seals are an important component in industrial equipment and are used to prevent leakage of fluids from pumps, compressors, and other machinery. India has a well-established mechanical seal industry.
